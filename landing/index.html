FAQ

Common questions

How long does setup take?
Under 5 minutes. Add one webhook URL to your Stripe dashboard. No code required on your end.
Does BillingWatch store my payment data?
No. BillingWatch only processes Stripe webhook event metadata — event types, timestamps, amounts. No card numbers or PII are stored. Ever.
What Stripe plan do I need?
Any Stripe account. BillingWatch uses webhooks, which are available on all Stripe plans including free.
Can I customize the detection thresholds?
Yes, on the Pro plan. Adjust failure rate thresholds, revenue drop percentages, and time windows to match your actual business patterns.
What happens if BillingWatch goes down?
Stripe retries webhook events for 3 days. BillingWatch will process queued events when it comes back online — events are never lost.
Can I get Slack alerts on the free plan?
Email only on the free plan. Slack, webhooks, and PagerDuty are Pro and Team features. Upgrade anytime — no contracts, cancel anytime.
