## Email 2: How Anomaly Detection Actually Works

**Subject:** We caught a billing bug before your customer did

Hey [Name],

Here's a real scenario we see all the time:

A developer updates a pricing plan in Stripe, but doesn't realize the proration logic is off. New customers start getting charged incorrectly. Nobody notices for 3 days — until a customer emails in angry.

With BillingWatch: the anomaly shows up within the first billing cycle. You get an alert. You fix it before it becomes a support ticket, a refund, or a churn event.

**Tips to get the most out of it:**

- Set your alert thresholds based on your average transaction size — tighter thresholds = earlier warnings
- Use the historical view to baseline what "normal" looks like for your billing patterns
- Enable webhook alerts if you want to push notifications into your existing monitoring stack

If you haven't connected your Stripe account yet, do it now — that's where the value lives.
