# HN Commenter Follow-Up DM Templates

**When to use:** 24-48 hours after Show HN post. Target commenters who engaged positively but didn't sign up.
**Where to send:** HN doesn't have DMs. Use email if visible in their profile, or Twitter/X if linked.
**Tone:** Personal, short, zero pitch. You're offering help, not selling.

---

## Template A: Technical Interest (they asked about architecture/detectors)

Subject: re: your BillingWatch question

Hey {name},

Saw your comment on the BillingWatch Show HN about {specific topic}. Good question — I didn't want to write a wall of text in the thread so figured I'd reach out directly.

{1-2 sentence answer to their specific question}

If you're dealing with anything similar on your Stripe setup, happy to help you think through it. No pitch — just been deep in this problem space for a while.

— Ray

## Rules

1. **Never batch-send.** Each DM is personalized with something from their actual comment.
2. **Max 10 DMs per launch.** Quality over quantity. Pick the most engaged commenters.
3. **No follow-up if they don't reply.** One message, that's it.
4. **No links in the first message** unless they specifically asked for one.
5. **If they reply, switch to conversation mode.** Drop the template, be human.
