Here is a Product Hunt launch checklist for BillingWatch:

**Pre-Launch**

* **Optimal Launch Timing**: Schedule the launch for 12:01 AM PST on [Date] to maximize visibility and engagement.
* **Gallery Assets**
	+ Logo design in PNG, SVG, and JPEG formats
	+ Screenshots of:
		- Dashboard with key features highlighted
		- Anomaly alerts and notifications
* **Tagline Variants**: Prepare 3 tagline options under 60 characters:
	1. "Catch billing bugs before they bite"
	2. "Watch your Stripe bills like a hawk"
	3. "Detect anomalies, avoid disasters"

**Hunter Profile Preparation**

* Ensure the Hunter profile is complete with:
	+ Clear profile picture
	+ Compelling bio that highlights expertise in SaaS and/or Stripe
	+ Relevant badges or certifications (e.g., Stripe Partner)
* Set up notifications to respond promptly to comments and messages

**Launch Day**

* **First Comment Script**: Prepare a response to the first comment, sharing the founder's story about encountering billing bugs:
	+ "Thanks for your question! I'm [Founder Name], one of the creators of BillingWatch. We built this tool after experiencing firsthand the frustration of dealing with unexpected charges on our own Stripe account. Our goal is to help others avoid those same headaches."
* **Day-1 Engagement Strategy**
	+ Respond to every comment within the first 2 hours:
		- Engage with users, ask questions, and clarify any doubts
		- Address concerns and provide more information as needed
	+ Upvote strategy:
		- Upvote at least 5 relevant comments in the first hour
		- Focus on high-quality comments that add value to the discussion

**Post-Launch**

* Monitor the launch thread for feedback, questions, and suggestions
* Engage with users on Product Hunt and other social media channels
* Share user testimonials or success stories as they come in
