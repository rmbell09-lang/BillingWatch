When BillingWatch is the right choice

BillingWatch isn't trying to replace Baremetrics. It's solving a different, more specific problem: catching billing anomalies before your customers notice them.

🎯

You need anomaly detection, not dashboards

If your main goal is to know when something breaks — duplicate charges, failed webhooks, sudden refund spikes — BillingWatch is purpose-built for that. Baremetrics is built for MRR tracking.

🔒

Your billing data stays on your servers

Stripe event data includes customer emails, amounts, and subscription states. With BillingWatch, that data never leaves your infrastructure. With Baremetrics or ProfitWell, it does.

🚀

You're early-stage and $199/mo hurts

When you're at $5K MRR, paying $199/mo for metrics tooling is a 4% overhead hit. BillingWatch gives you real-time billing monitoring for free until you scale to where Baremetrics makes sense.

🛠️

You want to own and extend your tooling

BillingWatch is MIT-licensed Python. Read the code, add detectors, change alert thresholds. No black boxes, no vendor lock-in.

When Baremetrics or ProfitWell is the better choice

Honest take: if you need full SaaS metrics — MRR, ARR, LTV, churn cohorts, forecasting — Baremetrics and ProfitWell are significantly more complete than BillingWatch. BillingWatch doesn't try to compete on metrics dashboards. It competes on anomaly detection and cost.

  • You need MRR/ARR/LTV forecasting — Baremetrics has far deeper financial modeling
  • You need customer-level revenue history — ProfitWell's customer profiles are detailed
  • You need churn recovery flows — ProfitWell Retain has active dunning management
  • Your team doesn't want to self-host — fully managed SaaS is worth money for some teams
